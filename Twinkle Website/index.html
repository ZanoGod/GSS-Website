Join Twinkle University

Twinkle University was established as a Japanese Language Centre since 2019 and providing Japanese Language Proficiency Courses.

We further expanded to providing professional certification courses and skill trainings such as nurse aid trainings and IT certification courses.

  • Level 3 Diploma in Computing (L3DC)
  • Level 4 Diploma in Computing (L4DC)
  • Level 5 Diploma in Computing (L5DC)

In 2020, Twinkle University has become accredited partner of NCC Education, UK and currently we are offering NCC Education Program Diploma Level 3, Level 4 and Level 5 Courses. https://www.nccedu.com/study-centres/twinkle-university/ Further to this, Twinkle University is actively delivering career coaching program for networking professionals who wants to develop their career abroad. In 2022, Twinkle University has also become Academia Partner of EC-Council and started providing wide range of cyber security courses.

Why Choose Twinkle?

At Twinkle University, students can get B.Sc (Hons) by attending Level 6 Top-up Degrees which is a Top-up Degrees Level of NCC Education UK.
