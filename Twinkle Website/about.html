About Us

Welcome to our Twinkle University

We are going to introduce about how “Twinkle University” started and the kinds of programs teaching at the university. Established as “Twinkle Language Center” in (2019), Japanese Language and Nurse Aid started teaching. After this, we connected the trainees with the Agency so that trainees could go to Japan to work as Nurse Aids.

In order to work with Networking Career in Singapore, we teach necessary technical skills from the basics of Networking to obtaining Global certificates. To pass the global certificates such as CCNA, CCNP, JNCIA, PCNSE, which are necessary to go and work internationally, we teach not only theory but also combined with Lab Sessions to effectively in the workplace. After obtaining the required certificates, we are assisting trainees until they get a job interview in Singapore, including English writing, according to with “Career Coaching Program”. In (2020), Twinkle University became “Accredited Partner” of NCC UK Education. Later, every young person interested in IT was able to attend Twinkle University according to the level from "Foundation Diploma Level" to "Bachelor Degree Level". Started from Level 3 Diploma to Level 6 Diploma (Bachelor), Grade 9 students or anyone 16 years of age or older can be attended and can become IT professionals. In 2022, Twinkle University joined together with EC-Council and became an "Academia Partner". All young people interested in Cyber Security in Myanmar can attend CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker) Course, EC-Council's Entry-level Cybersecurity Program, called C|CT (Certified Cybersecurity Technician) Course and CND (Certified Network Defender) Course at Twinkle University, which is an Academia Partner with fair price.
